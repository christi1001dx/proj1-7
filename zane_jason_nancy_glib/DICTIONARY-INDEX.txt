USER DICTIONARY:
{'name': <string username>, 'password': <string password>, 'stories': <list of story ids>, 'lines':<list of line ids>}

STORY DICTIONARY:
{'author': <user id>, 'text': <list of line ids; first will be title, the rest the text>, 'timestamp': <datetime object>, 'completed':<boolean>}

LINE DICTIONARY:
{'author': <user id>, 'text':<string>, 'timestamp':<UTC datetime object>'selected':<boolean>}
